Introduction

The Bean Zone

The Bean Zone is a local coffee company specializing in unique blends from specialty coffee bean growers.

Target audience

The business is directly targeting individuals are passionate about the quality of their coffee over the delivery and price. These are likely millennials with median to high income.

Due to the fact that many are currently unable to purchase quality coffee from local outlets, this website will serve to substitute the experience through an online storefront, prioritizing both convenience and innovation
